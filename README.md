# Overview

AgentFlow AI is an intelligent multi-agent research platform that automates the complete research workflow.

Simply provide a topic and the system will:

- Search the web for relevant information.
- Extract and summarize website content.
- Generate a structured research report.
- Critically evaluate the generated report.
- Track the entire pipeline in real-time.
- Save research history to the database.
- Export professional PDF reports.
- Present results through an interactive dashboard.

# AI Architecture

The project follows a Multi-Agent architecture consisting of:

### Search Agent

Responsible for:

- Searching the web.
- Gathering relevant resources.
- Identifying valuable information.

---

### Content Agent

Responsible for:

- Extracting webpage content.
- Summarizing information.
- Preparing research context.

---

### Writer Chain

Responsible for:

- Generating professional research reports.
- Structuring content.
- Creating comprehensive analyses.

---

### Critic Chain

Responsible for:

- Evaluating the generated report.
- Identifying strengths and weaknesses.
- Providing improvement suggestions.
- Assigning a quality score.
